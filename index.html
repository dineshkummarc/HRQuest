Our Features

HR Quest for Work

With HR Quest, you can track each employee's work day and productivity, log working hours and keep track of remaining time off and grant leaves for employee.

HR Quest for Talent Management

You can also find and recruit top talent, organize employee onboarding and offboarding, keep track of employee information.

HR Quest for Payroll

Last but not least, you can generate payroll of particular employee based on their attendance.
